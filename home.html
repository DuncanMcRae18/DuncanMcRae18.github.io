About me:

I grew up on a small farm in the little town of Bainsville, Ontario. Growing up I learned a great deal about work ethic and gained a mechanical inclination from helping my family maintain and operate various types of farm equipment. I was always interested in how things worked, especially electronic systems, so I began my path through post-secondary education at the University of Ottawa in 2021.

As mentioned, I'm currently in my second-last course-based semester at the University of Ottawa (although I have two CO-OP terms left). and as part of my program I am searching for CO-OP opportunities that align with my interests and skills. I chose to also major in physics as I am ever curious as to why things work the way they do, and to suppliment my fascination with electricity and with a specialization in communications.

In my free time I enjoy working on personal projects such as this website and microcontroller systems, having completed a few projects in those areas during my coursework and on my own time. Like any good farm boy, I also love to visit home and help out; not to mention the constant supply of things to fix and tinker with!
